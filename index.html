The Problem: What Changed in 2021?

In 2021, the Federal Government removed e-bikes from the Motor Vehicle Standards Act. This action, intended to encourage micromobility, removed the requirement for imports to meet key international safety compliance standards (like EN15194).

Why It Matters:

  • Unsafe imports with poor battery design leading to house fires.
  • Crashes and fatalities involving non-compliant, overpowered machines.
  • Youth riders using motorcycle-style e-bikes at dangerous speeds.
  • Lack of consistent regulation and consumer protection across Australia.
