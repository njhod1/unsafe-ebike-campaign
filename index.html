Protect Australians from Unsafe E-Bikes

Join the campaign to reverse the 2021 exemption that allowed dangerous imports.

What Changed in 2021?

In 2021, the Federal Government removed e-bikes from the Motor Vehicle Standards Act. This allowed imports without international safety compliance, leading to unsafe, overpowered e-bikes flooding the market.

Why It Matters

  • Unsafe imports with poor battery design leading to **battery fires**
  • Crashes and fatalities involving non-compliant machines
  • Overpowered e-bikes capable of speeds well beyond 25 km/h used illegally by youth riders
